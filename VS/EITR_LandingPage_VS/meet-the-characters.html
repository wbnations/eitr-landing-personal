Meet the Characters

Five professionals. Five elephants. One shared journey.

The Cast

EP 01

Gerald

IT Manager

His Elephant

"If I tell my team to use AI tools, what happens when they work better without me?"

Backstory

Gerald has managed IT support teams for 11 years. He built his value on knowing every system, every workaround, every team member by name. He's the person people call when everything breaks — and he's never failed to fix it. That identity is at stake.

What Changed

When he finally uses AI to manage a crisis, he realizes it doesn't replace his judgment — it amplifies it. The instinct that took 11 years to develop becomes the thing that makes him better at using the tool, not the thing the tool replaces.

EP 02

Shae

Business Leader

Her Elephant

"I didn't get to the executive table by outsourcing my thinking."

Backstory

Shae leads a 60-person division and has been the smartest person in most rooms she's entered. She's built a career on precision and instinct — and she's deeply suspicious of any tool that makes hard things look easy.

What Changed

Her board presentation becomes the test case she didn't volunteer for. The results make her rethink what "her thinking" actually means — and whether protecting the process was ever really the point.

EP 03

Keith

Student / Athlete

His Elephant

"Using AI to study feels like cheating. Or at least like giving up."

Backstory

Keith is a sophomore on an athletic scholarship managing 18 credits, practice, film study, and travel weekends. He's been told his whole life that hard work beats shortcuts — and he believes it, to a fault.

What Changed

He stops seeing AI as a shortcut and starts seeing it as a system — like film study, but for academics. The discipline he already has becomes the thing that makes him good at it.

EP 04

Izzy

IT Professional

Her Elephant

"I write the code. I'm not going to let code write me."

Backstory

Izzy is a mid-level software engineer who prides herself on clean, human-written code. She's watched colleagues adopt AI tools and produce what she considers "slop" — and she's made it her identity not to be one of them.

What Changed

A code review gone sideways forces her to confront whether her standard is about quality — or ego. The answer is uncomfortable. And useful.

Watch Her Episode
EP 05

Glenn

Sales Professional

His Elephant

"Sales is a relationship game. You can't automate trust."

Backstory

Glenn has 14 years in sales and has always believed the deal is won in the room, on the call, in the handshake. AI feels like a threat to the one thing that's always set him apart — knowing people.

What Changed

He discovers AI isn't replacing the relationship — it's giving him the context to make every touchpoint count more. The calls get shorter. The close rate doesn't.
