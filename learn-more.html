What Is the AI Skills Navigator?

The AI Skills Navigator is a role-based learning system built to move people from AI avoidance to AI fluency — in the flow of real work. Rather than generic AI literacy courses, the Navigator maps specific skills to specific roles: what an IT manager needs is different from what a sales professional, student, or business leader needs.

Each character in Elephant In The Room follows a real Navigator path — Foundations, Proficiency, and Expertise. The content you see in the show is the content you can learn from yourself. No prerequisites. No certification track required. Just the next skill that moves you forward in the role you already have.

Three Tiers. One Path Forward.

Tier 1

Role Foundations

Build practical habits specific to your role. Start seeing results in your first week. No theory — just the skills that apply to your actual work, today.

Tier 2

Role Proficiency

Move from one-off prompts to repeatable workflows. AI becomes part of how you work, not an extra task on top of it. This is where habits become systems.

Tier 3

Role Expertise

Lead, govern, and scale AI adoption. Become the person your team looks to — not because you took a course, but because you've done the work at every tier.
